Instructions


Welcome to Guess The Number.

In this game, a number will be randomly generated, and you will have to guess the hidden number correctly within the allocated number of tries and time given, in order to earn points.
Rules:
  • You are given a fixed number of tries per game to guess the hidden number.
  • Each game will have a time limit of 60 seconds.
  • For each correct guess, you will automatically earn at least 100 points (increases with a higher level).
  • Additionally, you can earn extra points if you guess the number more quickly / have more remaining unused tries.
  • The game ends when you use up all the tries / all the time given but fail to guess the number correctly.
  • To help you, a range of numbers will be provided, and the hidden number will fall within this range of numbers.
  • Additionally, each time you make a wrong guess, the game will inform you whether the hidden number is higher or lower than your guess.
  • As you guess more numbers correctly, you will level up. This comes with harder guessing problems (larger range of numbers), but also more tries and increased score earned.
